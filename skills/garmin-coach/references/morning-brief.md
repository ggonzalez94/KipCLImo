# Morning Brief Template

Pull:

- `garmin sleep <today>`
- `garmin hrv <today>`
- `garmin training-readiness <today>`
- `garmin training-status <today>`
- `garmin body-battery <yesterday> <today>`
- `garmin stress <yesterday>`
- `garmin activities --start <yesterday> --end <today> --limit 5`

Output shape:

1. Sleep headline: sleep score, duration, one notable stage or recovery detail.
2. Recovery read: HRV versus baseline or recent average, readiness score, body battery context.
3. Training context: status, load, and whether yesterday’s work changed the picture.
4. Today’s nudge: push, maintain, or protect recovery.

Style:

- Keep it to 4-5 sentences.
- Be conversational, compact, and data-dense.
- For unattended delivery, write it in Spanish.
